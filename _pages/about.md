Hello! I am currently a 3rd year PhD student at Kansas State University, where I am fortunately advised by [Dinh-Liem Nguyen](http://sites.google.com/site/dinhliemnguyen/home) . My interest revolves around applying machine learning techniques to solve inverse problems. I also have interests in the theoretical understanding of deep learning through mathematical theory and more broadly, in statistical learning theory. You can contact me at my email: arav0006[at]ksu[dot]edu.
